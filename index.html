⚙️ Simulation Parameters

Test different scenarios by changing asteroid properties

Asteroid size directly affects damage amount. 10m: window breakage, 1000m+: mass destruction.
Earth's escape velocity is 11 km/s. Higher speeds create larger explosions.
Stone: ~2500, Iron: ~7800 kg/m³. Dense asteroids carry more kinetic energy.
Vertical impact (90°) maximizes damage concentration. Shallow angles distribute effects over wider areas.

📚 Asteroid Science

What you need to know about asteroids

🌌

What Are Asteroids?

Rocky remnants left over from solar system formation. Most are found in the belt between Mars and Jupiter.

📊

Statistics

Approximately 1 million asteroids are known. Those larger than 100m+ can destroy cities.

🔭

Detection Systems

More than 20 telescope systems on Earth continuously scan the sky searching for asteroids.

⚡

Impact Risk

100m asteroid hits Earth every 10,000 years, 1km asteroid every 500,000 years.
