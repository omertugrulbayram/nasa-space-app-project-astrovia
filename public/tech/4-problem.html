PROBLEM
The Problem:
• Every year, thousands of asteroids pass near Earth
• Some potentially dangerous
• Detecting them requires time, expertise, and powerful equipment
Our Experience:
• We previously joined NASA's IASC Asteroid Search Campaign
• Discovered 6 provisional asteroids, featured in world final reports
• This taught us how challenging and time-consuming detection can be
Our Motivation:
To make the discovery process faster, accessible, and educational for everyone
